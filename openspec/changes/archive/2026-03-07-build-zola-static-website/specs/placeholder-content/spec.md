## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: About section content
The homepage SHALL include an About section with Lorem Ipsum placeholder text.

#### Scenario: About section exists
- **WHEN** homepage renders
- **THEN** About section heading is displayed

#### Scenario: Lorem ipsum content
- **WHEN** About section renders
- **THEN** placeholder Lorem Ipsum text is displayed

### Requirement: Research Interests section
The homepage SHALL include a Research Interests section with bullet points.

#### Scenario: Research Interests exists
- **WHEN** homepage renders
- **THEN** Research Interests section with placeholder interests is displayed

### Requirement: Working Papers section
The homepage SHALL include a Working Papers section with placeholder content.

#### Scenario: Working Papers section exists
- **WHEN** homepage renders
- **THEN** Working Papers section with Lorem Ipsum is displayed

### Requirement: Other section
The homepage SHALL include an Other section for blogs, awards, etc. with placeholder content.

#### Scenario: Other section exists
- **WHEN** homepage renders
- **THEN** Other section with placeholder content is displayed
